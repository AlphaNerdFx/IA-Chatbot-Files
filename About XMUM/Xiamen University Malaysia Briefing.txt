Xiamen University Malaysia (XMUM) Briefing
This briefing provides an overview of Xiamen University Malaysia (XMUM), the first university branch campus from China, based on the "XMUM General Brochure 2024.pdf" source.

1. Overview of Xiamen University (XMU)
Xiamen University (XMU), the parent institution of XMUM, was "founded in 1921 by Mr. Tan Kah Kee, a renowned overseas Chinese entrepreneur and philanthropist in Southeast Asia." It holds historical significance as "the first Chinese higher educational institution established by an overseas Chinese."

XMU is highly regarded within China and globally:

National Recognition: It receives "privileged support from China’s Ministry of Education" through "Project 211" (developing 100 priority institutions for the 21st century) and "Project 985" (building world-class universities). Since 2014, XMU has hosted two collaborative innovation centres of "Project 2011" (Higher Education Innovation Capacity Improvement Scheme).
Rankings (2023):
US News and World Report: "21st in China", "41st in Asia", "247th Globally".
Nature Index 2023: "15th in China", "35th Globally".
Academic Excellence: "11 disciplines rank among the TOP 1% globally" in Essential Science Indicators (ESI), including Chemistry, Engineering, Materials Science, Clinical Medicine, Plant and Animal Science, Environment/Ecology, Biology and Biochemistry, Physics, Mathematics, Agricultural Sciences, and Social Sciences, General.
Campus: Ranked "One of the 14 most beautiful campuses in the world" by The Daily Telegraph (UK).
Statistics: XMU has "27 Schools", "76 Departments", "10 Research Institutions", "2703 Full-time academics", and "40,465 students including more than 2,600 overseas students". It partners with "280 Foreign partner universities and colleges for exchange and" programmes.
2. Establishment and Vision of Xiamen University Malaysia (XMUM)
XMUM was established "Upon the invitation of the Malaysian Ministry of Higher Education to set up a branch campus in Malaysia." It is notable as "the pioneer university from China to establish a branch campus in Malaysia," embodying the vision of its founder, Mr. Tan Kah Kee.

XMUM's aspiration is "to become a university with a distinct global outlook, featuring first-class teaching and research, and embracing cultural diversity."

3. XMUM Campus and Facilities
Location: The Malaysia campus is in "Bandar Sunsuria, Sepang, Selangor Darul Ehsan," conveniently located a "15-minute drive from the Kuala Lumpur International Airport and a 25-minute drive from Putrajaya."
Investment and Size: It is a "purpose-built campus occupying an area of 150 acres with an estimated total investment of RM 1.3 billion." The total planned floor space is "470,000 m2", with a total capacity for "10,000 students."
Infrastructure: The brochure features images of modern academic buildings, a large library, sports facilities (including a swimming pool, running track, basketball and tennis courts), and student accommodation.
Accommodation: On-campus student hostels are available to foster cooperation and facilitate participation in extra-curricular activities. Room types include "Twin-sharing (Block D)" (15.9 m², RM 340/month) and "Twin-sharing (Block LY)" (17.6 m², RM 390/month), both with en-suite bathrooms. Facilities in hostels include "Bed, study table, chair, wardrobe," "High-speed Wi-Fi connection," "Air-conditioner in every room (utility bill excluded)," "Common pantry on each floor... equipped with a microwave, refrigerator, kettle, and water dispenser," and "Laundry room."
4. Academic Programmes and Staff
XMUM offers a range of programmes at different levels:

Foundation: Arts and Social Science, Science.
Undergraduate:
Arts and Social Science: Chinese Studies, English Language and Literature, Journalism, Advertising, Communication.
Business: Accounting, Finance, International Business, E-commerce.
Information Technology: Computer Science and Technology, Digital Media Technology, Software Engineering, Artificial Intelligence, Cyber Security, Data Science.
Engineering: Chemical Engineering, Electrical and Electronics Engineering.
Engineering Technology: New Energy Science and Engineering.
Science: Marine Biotechnology, Marine Environmental Chemistry, Physics, Mathematics and Applied Mathematics.
Medicine: Traditional Chinese Medicine.
Master's: Business Administration, Chinese Studies, Communication and Culture, Finance, Chemical Engineering, Mathematics and Applied Mathematics, Marine Biotechnology, New Energy Science and Engineering, Electrical and Electronic Engineering, Physics. Some Master's programmes are offered in Mixed Mode or Research Mode.
PhD: Chinese Studies, Chemical Engineering, Mathematics, New Energy Science and Engineering, Physics, Information Technology. Some PhD programmes are offered in Mixed Mode or Research Mode.
Academic Staff: "80% of our academic staff are doctoral degree holders." Notably, "one-third of our academic staff are seconded from XMU China, while the rest are recruited from Malaysia and other countries." The student-to-lecturer ratio is "15:1".

5. Why Choose XMUM
XMUM highlights several benefits for prospective students:

International Education with Chinese Elements: Offers an "international educational opportunity imbued with distinctive Chinese elements."
Competitive Advantage: Provides a "competitive advantage in economic, cultural, and technological exchange between China and ASEAN countries."
Campus Environment: Features a "diverse campus culture coupled with state-of-the-art infrastructure."
Reputation: Students benefit from "A trusted and renowned Chinese university with a long history of academic excellence."
Affordability: Offers "Quality education at an affordable cost."
Further Studies: Provides "Opportunities to pursue postgraduate studies at XMUM or XMU China."
Financial Aids: "Merit-based scholarships and study grants are available."
Study Abroad: Offers "Student exchange or internship opportunities in China and other countries."
Qualification Recognition: XMUM degrees are "recognised internationally, including in China and Malaysia."
6. Application Process
Applicants can apply via:

Online Application: Through the official website.
E-mail Application: By downloading and sending the completed form and documents via email.
Paper Application: By sending printed forms and documents by hand or post to the Admissions Office in Sepang, Selangor Darul Ehsan, Malaysia.
Fees: A non-refundable application fee of RM100 and a non-refundable registration fee of RM200 are required. Payments can be made via cash, cheque, bank draft, or bank transfer to "XMU JIAGENG EDUCATION DEVELOPMENT SDN BHD" (Public Bank Berhad or Malayan Banking Berhad).

Supporting Documents: Required documents include NRIC (Malaysian) or Passport (international), certified true copies of certificates and transcripts, a certified true copy of the School Leaving Certificate, proof of fee payment, and a Scholarship & Study Grant Application Form (if applicable).
